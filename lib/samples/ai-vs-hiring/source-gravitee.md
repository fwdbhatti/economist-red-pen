# Gravitee — Cost Guide: Agentic AI Deployment Pricing and Planning

## What this article actually covers

This Gravitee guide is about the cost of building a **developer portal for an agentic AI deployment** — i.e. the API gateway, documentation, and authentication layer that fronts an AI mesh. It is not a cost guide for the AI agents themselves, nor a cost guide for sales-AI deployments specifically.

## Cost figures published

### Building a developer portal in-house

- Engineering hours: 3 to 6 full-time engineers
- Time to market: 6 to 12 months to launch
- Estimated upfront cost: **$300,000 to $600,000**
- Infrastructure and hosting: $5,000 to $15,000 monthly
- Maintenance and support: at least 1 to 2 FTE engineers, $120,000 to $200,000 annually
- Security and compliance: $50,000+ annually for audits and compliance updates

## Notes on this source vs. the draft article

- The draft article cites Gravitee as the source for "industry pricing for mid-market agentic AI deployments covering sales workflows (lead qualification, outreach, quote turnaround, pipeline progression) runs roughly £50k-£170k Year 1 all-in." **That figure does not appear in the Gravitee article.** Gravitee discusses developer portal costs ($300k-$600k upfront in-house build, $1M+ over 3 years) — a different cost category entirely.
- The Gravitee article is in USD; the draft converts to GBP without flagging the conversion.
- The Gravitee article is vendor marketing for an API/portal product, not a market study of sales-AI deployment pricing. Treating it as a benchmark source is a category error.
